About VANCROX

Vancrox is a technology-driven platform project designed to build a transparent digital ecosystem connecting investors with professional traders and advanced market analysis tools. The platform focuses on system clarity, structured participation and modern web infrastructure.

Our goal is to provide a secure and professional environment where users can interact with experienced traders and technology-assisted trading systems while maintaining transparency and operational integrity.

How Vancrox Works

Vancrox is designed as a digital platform where investors and experienced traders can interact within a structured and transparent system environment. The platform allows users to explore trader profiles and participate in market activities through professional traders or technology-assisted tools.

For Investors

Investors can create an account on the platform and explore available trader profiles. Each trader may provide information about their experience and trading performance.

Based on this information, investors can choose a trader they wish to work with and hire them directly through the platform interface.

For Traders

Experienced traders who wish to participate on the platform can submit their trading performance records or trading statements for verification. This process helps maintain transparency and ensures that traders participating on the platform have relevant trading experience.

After completing the verification process, traders may be able to offer their trading services through the platform and interact with investors.

Trader Compensation

Verified traders may receive compensation for their services based on their activity and participation within the platform. This may include service fees, performance-based earnings or structured compensation depending on platform conditions.

New User Protection

Vancrox provides a limited protection policy designed to support new users during their initial experience with the platform.

Refund eligibility applies only to new users and is limited to the first three trader interactions. If a loss occurs during these initial trades, the platform refund mechanism may activate according to platform rules.

Refund Policy

Vancrox provides a limited refund policy designed to support new users during their initial platform experience.

Refund eligibility applies only to new users and is limited to the first three trader interactions on the platform. If a loss occurs during these initial trades, the platform refund mechanism may activate according to the platform's internal rules and refund logic.

After the first three trader interactions, normal trading conditions and platform rules apply.
